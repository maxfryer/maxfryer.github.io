How to Play

Make tea for customers to earn tips!

  1. Click kettle to fill, then click again to boil
  2. Click a teabag (EG, EB, PM, CH) to add to cup
  3. Click boiled kettle to pour water and start steeping
  4. Add milk/sugar if the recipe needs it
  5. Click cup when ready, then click customer to serve

Recipes

  • Earl Grey (EG): 3s steep, no milk
  • English Breakfast (EB): 4s steep, milk required
  • Peppermint (PM): 2s steep, no milk, no sugar
  • Chai (CH): 5s steep, milk + sugar required

Press SPACE or P to resume
